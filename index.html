About Me

A Texas native, I spent the first part of my life in Corpus Christi, TX. After The University of Texas at Austin, graduating with a degree in Geological Sciences with a minor in Petroleum Engineering. After spending a few years in Los Angeles pursuing fine arts and developing an eye for meaningful design, I switched my focus to a life-long interest: coding. As a lover of aesthetics, I aim to create beautiful, functional sites with color psychology in mind.
